Giving is Virtue.

Love is always the answer.

Love

Majority of the time we find ourselves being compassionate towards an animal, that animal is domesticated, or a pet.People's love towards an animal should transcend such silly boundaries, only then can we truly understand the needs of stray animals and the pain they must feel.

Compassion

By showing empathy and putting forth a helping hand to strays in need. They might not say it but we understand. and it is this silence in our acknowledgement that can be the difference between thinking of saving a life, and actually doing it.

Endless Pawsabilities

With our team of hardworking and dedicated paw-patrols, we see endless pawsibilities, in a future where the streets of urban and rural areas are not classified by the "filth" of stray animals, but by the humanity that goes into helping them.

I really like the idea for the website and wholeheartedly support their endeavor."”
